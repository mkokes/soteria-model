# Amniga Qalabka

Telefoonkaagu waa barta ugu nugul. Haddii la qaado, wuxuu ka kooban yahay farriimo, xiriiriyaal, taariikhda goobta, iyo wax badan. Badbaadi.

## Xidhmada Taleefanka

### Daar Xidh Xoog leh

Telefoonkaagu waa inuu leeyahay shaashadda xidhan. Ikhtiyaarada:

| Habka | Xoogga | Xusuusinta |
|--------|----------|-------|
| PIN 6+ lambar | Wanaagsan | Ka fogow qaababka cad (123456, taariikhda dhalashada) |
| Erayga sirta ah ee xarfaha iyo lambarada | Wanaagsan | Adag in la arko |
| Bayoomeetariga + PIN | Ugu wanaagsan | Fudud laakiin lagu qasbi karaa |

::: warning BAYOOMEETARIGA IYO SHARCIGA
Goobaha qaarkood, waxaa lagugu qasbi karaa inaad furto telefoonkaaga faraha ama wejiga, laakiin maaha PIN. Ka fiirso in la joojiyoبayoomeetariga xaalado khatar sare leh.
:::

### Xidh Otomaatig ah

U habee telefoonkaaga inuu si degdeg ah u xidho:
- **iPhone**: Settings → Display → Auto-Lock → 30 ilbiriqsi
- **Android**: Settings → Security → Screen Lock → Auto-lock → 15-30 ilbiriqsi

### Xidh Degdeg ah

Ogow sida si degdeg ah loo xidho/joojiyoقalabkaaga:
- **iPhone**: Riix badhanka dhinaca 5 jeer (u baahan PIN, joojiyaa Face ID)
- **Android**: Way kala duwan tahay qalabka — baro kaaga

## Sirta

Taleefannada casriga ah waa kuwo sir ah markii hore, laakiin xaqiiji:

- **iPhone**: Si otomaatig ah loo daar markii aad dejiso furaha
- **Android**: Settings → Security → Encryption (waa inuu dhahaa "Encrypted")

Sirtu waxay ka dhigan tahay in xogta aan la akhriyi karin PIN-kaaga la'aan — laakiin kaliya haddii telefoonkaagu xidhan yahay.

## Habaynta Signal

Signal waa qalabkeenna ugu muhiimsan ee isgaarsiinta. U habee amniga:

### Dejinta Loo baahan yahay

Fur Signal → Settings → Privacy:

- [x] **Screen Lock**: U baahan PIN/bayoomeetariga si loo furo Signal
- [x] **Screen Security**: Xannib sawirrada shaashadda
- [x] **Registration Lock**: Ka hor weerarrada beddelka SIM
- [ ] **Read Receipts**: Ka fiirso OFF (yareeya metadata)
- [ ] **Typing Indicators**: Ka fiirso OFF

### Farriimaha Libdhaya

Deji caadi ahaan dhammaan wada hadalada:
- Signal Settings → Privacy → Default Disappearing Messages → **1 toddobaad** (ama ka yar)

Wada hadalada xasaasiga ah, isticmaal 1 maalin ama 1 saac.

### Xaqiijinta Lambarka Amniga

Xaqiiji lambarrada amniga xiriiriyaasha muhiimka ah (shakhsi ahaan ama wicitaanka fiidiyowga):
1. Fur wada hadal
2. Taabo magaca xiriirka
3. Taabo "View Safety Number"
4. Barbar dhig lambarada

Tani waxay xaqiijisaa inaad la hadlayso qofka dhabta ah, maaha mid been ah.

## Apps Kale

### Ka saar Apps aan Loo baahnayn

Apps waxay ururiyaan xogta. Kaliya hay waxaad u baahan tahay.

### Dib u eeg Ogolaanshaha Apps

Si joogto ah u hubi apps ay helaan:
- Goobta
- Xiriiriyaasha
- Kamaradda/Makarafoonka
- Kaydka

Ka saar ogolaanshaha aan loo baahnayn.

### Adeegyada Goobta

Noqo mid ula jeedo goobta:
- Jooji goobta apps aan u baahnayn
- Ka fiirso in si buuxda loo joojiyoقalabka xilli hawlo xasaasi ah
- Xusuuso: taleefannada weli waxaa lagu heli karaa munaaradaha gacanta

## Kaabka Daruuraha

### Khatar

Kaabka daruuraha (iCloud, Google) waxaa geli kara hay'adaha sharciga habka sharciga saxda ah. Tan waxaa ka mid ah taariikhda farriimaha.

### Signal iyo Kaabka

- **iOS**: Farriimaha Signal KUMA jiraan kaabka iCloud (wanaagsan)
- **Android**: Signal wuxuu leeyahay nidaamka kaabka sirta ah — ha isticmaalin kan Google

### Ka fiirso Joojinta Kaabka Daruuraha

Amniga ugu sarreeya, jooji kaabka daruuraha si buuxda — laakiin ogow inaad lumin doonto xogta haddii aad lumiso telefoonkaaga.

## Qalabka Hawlaha Unugga

### Qalab Gooni ah (Fiican)

Haddii suurtogal tahay, isticmaal telefoon gooni ah hawlaha unugga:
- Telefoon hore loo bixiyay lacag caddaan ah
- Loo diiwaangeliyay lambarka Google Voice ama mid la mid ah
- Ka kooban kaliya Signal iyo apps loo baahan yahay
- Waligaa lama xiriirin aqoonsigaaga

### Qalab La wadaago (Runta)

Dadka badankood waxay isticmaalaan telefoonkooda gaarka ah. Waa hagaag haddii aad:
- Raacdo dhammaan dhaqannada amniga si adag
- Ku raaxaysan khatar
- Hay hawlaha unugga oo ka go'an noloshaaga gaarka ah

## Haddii Telefoonkaaga Lumay ama la Qabtay

2. U qaado in dhammaan waxa telefoonka ku jira la heli karo
3. Xubnaha kale waa in la ogeysiiyaa haddii loo baahan yahay
