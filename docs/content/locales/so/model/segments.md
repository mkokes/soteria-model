# Qaybaha

**Qaybaha** waa kooxo takhasusay oo unug gudihiisa ah, mid kasta oo leh shaqo gaar ah. Sifooyinka amniga muhiimka ah: **qaybaha way ka go'doomisan yihiin midba midka kale**.

## Qaybaha Aasaasiga ah

Kuwani waa qaybaha aasaasiga ah. Unugyadu waxay la qabsan karaan baahiyaha maxalliga ah.

### Waardiyayaasha (Daawashada & Jawaabta Degdegga ah)

**Ujeedada**: La socodsiinta iyo soo sheegista hawlaha shakiga leh si loo ilaaliyo bulshada.

**Hawlaha**:
- Ilaalinta hawlaha ilaalinta iyo fulinta sharciga
- Bixinta digniin hore iyada oo loo marayo wadooyin badbaado ah
- Uga jawaabida wicitaannada caawimaadda inta lagu jiro kulammada
- Dukumiintiyeynta isdhexgalka (ogolaanshaha)

**Weligood kuma laha**: Gaadiidka, qaybinta kheyraadka, ama aqoonta goobaha badbaadada.

---

### Gaadiidiyayaasha

**Ujeedada**: Bixinta safarro badbaado ah meel muhiim ah.

**Hawlaha**:
- Wadida shaqada, ballamaha caafimaadka, hawlaha
- Soo qaadista carruurta iskuulka
- Gaadiidka ballamaha sharciga
- Keenista raashinka iyo agabka

**Weligood kuma laha**: Hawlaha daawashada, aqoonta goobaha guryaha badbaadada.

---

### Shabakadda Kheyraadka

**Ujeedada**: Ururinta iyo qaybinta agabka, lacagta iyo waxyaabaha muhiimka ah.

**Hawlaha**:
- Abaabul ururinta cuntada iyo agabka
- Maaraynta lacagta degdegga ah
- Isku-dubaridka caawimaadda is-caawinta maxalliga ah
- Qaybinta iyada oo loo marayo wadooyin la aaminsan yahay

**Weligood kuma laha**: Daawashada, waddooyinka gaadiidka, goobaha guryaha badbaadada.
