## Isticmaalka Kheyraadkan

### Dadka Shakhsi ahaaneed

Haddii aad wax ka baranayso qaabka ama aad ka fikirayso bilowga unug:

1. Ku billow **[Qaabka](../model/)** si aad u fahamto qaab-dhismeedka
2. Dib u eeg **[Dhaqannada Amniga](../security/)** si aad u fahamto amniga hawlgalka
3. Sahami **[Qalabka Farsamada](./technical)** si aad u aragto goobaha aad isticmaali doonto
4. Soo deji **[Kaardka Xuquuqda](../rights/print-cards)** si aad ula socoto

### Kooxaha Aasaasiga

Haddii aad bilowday unug 3-5 qof oo la isku halayn karo:

1. Dhamaystir **[Hagaha Kooxda Aasaasiga](../start-a-cell/founding-team)**
2. Deji **[Signal](../start-a-cell/signal-setup)** dhammaan aasaasayaasha
3. Isticmaal **[Liisaska Hubinta La Daabici Karo](./printable)** si aad u raacdo horumarinta
4. Dib u eeg **[Habka Soo Dhaweynta](../start-a-cell/onboarding)** ka hor inta aadan ku darin xubnaha

### Unugyada Firfircoon

Haddii aad maamulay unug la dhisay:

1. Samee **[Hubinta Amniga](./printable#security-audit-checklist)** bishii
2. Isticmaal **[Qaababka](./printable)** habab joogto ah
3. Qaybso **[Kaardka Xuquuqda](../rights/print-cards)** xubnaha bulshada
4. Dib u eeg **[Dhaqannada Amniga](../security/)** si joogto ah dhammaan xubnaha
